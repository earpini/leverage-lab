they describe the natural output when the moves are right.

## Diachronic note (2010 → 2022)

- **2010 travel (19):** earnest, inflated, uniform sentences (lowest variance, std 7.8), no play, no
  reader address. The *before* — the youthful inflation to avoid.
- **2011 fiction (20):** rhythm arrives — high variance, cinematic fragments, scene-led, anaphora beats.
- **2015 essays (24):** the voice crystallizes — register collision, reader address, question→fragment,
  excavation, open landings.
- **2022 thesis (27):** same DNA in formal register — variance, named specifics, governing image,
  warmth in frame — play stripped, discipline gained.

Stable throughout: a human in the frame, concrete scene-staging, wide sentence-length variance (from
2011 on). Matured: register collision and reader address (by 2015); youthful inflation traded for
control.
